Why become a PocketMoni Agent ?

PocketMoni allows an individual or organization who becomes an agent to serve as a “mini-bank” in
his vicinity and conduct financial transactions anytime, anywhere, from his mobile phone using
either the USSD code *389#, the PocketMoni App or the PocketMoni POS.

Earn quick cash

PocketMoni empowers its agents to make extra income by profiling and activation on the POS scheme.
